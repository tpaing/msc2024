Future Direction

Modern broadcasting is shifting toward IP-based workflows where coding and networking are essential. Traditional broadcast operators are being replaced by technical managers who understand both production and software engineering.

My goal is to design scalable, automated esports broadcast systems where APIs, Unreal Engine, and live production tools work together seamlessly. The future of esports broadcasting is data-driven, automated, and built on software infrastructure—not just hardware and manual operation.

By combining broadcast engineering expertise with programming skills, I create systems that are not only reliable but also intelligent, reducing human error while increasing production quality and efficiency.
